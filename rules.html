General Guidelines

Rules

  • •   The registrants up to the age of 26 years are eligible for participation and prizes. A valid government photo-identification proof is required.
  • •   You can apply as a team of 1-5 members.
  • •   Once a team has submitted their idea, no new members can be added/removed from the team.
  • •   Only one idea can be submitted per team. Multiple entries will be disqualified.
  • •   Usage of English is mandatory during product pitching sessions.
  • •   There are 3 themes of the Ideathon, you must submit a idea that is pertaining to one of these themes.
  • •   The participants should have a good Internet connection during the event.
  • •   Ideas should be original and unique.
  • •   We welcome attendees from all backgrounds, genders and geographies. This event is about increasing the overall amount of innovation, partnership, and engagement in the developer community.


Disqualifications

  • •   Ideathon organizers have the right at their sole discretion to determine whether an entrant is eligible for the Contest. They may disqualify Ideathon entries (including, without limitation, content submitted in connection therewith) that (a) are in violation of third party rights or law or regulation, (b) use inappropriate or explicit language or images, or display content that Ideathon or Sponsors otherwise find objectionable.
  • •   Copyright violations, cheating, recklessness and endangering others, disrespectful behaviour, immoral acts, illegal acts etc. are strictly not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
